TourLink Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2, 2026

TourLink (“we”, “our”, or “us”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when using the TourLink mobile application (the “App”). By signing up or using our App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you register or use TourLink, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Full Name: Name and surname to personalize your experience.
  • Email Address: Used for account creation, notifications, and communication.
  • Password: Encrypted and securely stored for account access.
  • Country: Helps customize content and services for your location.
  • Age: To ensure age-appropriate access and services.
  • Gender: Optional, used for personalization and analytics.
  • Location Data: GPS or device-based location to provide location-specific services, maps, tours, and recommendations.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Creating and managing your TourLink account.
  • Providing and improving our services.
  • Personalizing your app experience based on your preferences and location.
  • Showing nearby points of interest, tours, or events relevant to your location.
  • Communicating important updates, notifications, and promotions.
  • Complying with legal obligations and protecting our rights.

3. Location Data

  • Collection: TourLink may collect your real-time location when the App is in use or in the background if you allow it.
  • Usage: Location data is used to provide personalized tours, nearby recommendations, route planning, and relevant notifications.
  • Control: You can enable or disable location access in your device settings at any time.

4. Data Security

TourLink implements reasonable security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption: Passwords, location, and sensitive information are encrypted.
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on secure servers with limited access.
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel have access to your data for operational purposes.

5. Sharing of Information

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors who assist with app functionality, hosting, analytics, or map/location services.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or to protect the rights and safety of TourLink, our users, or others.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

6. Your Rights

  • Access & Correction: View and update your profile information anytime.
  • Account Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data.
  • Location Control: You may disable location access anytime in your device settings.
  • Opt-Out of Communications: Unsubscribe from promotional emails at any time.

For any requests, contact us at privacy@tourlink.com.

7. Children’s Privacy

TourLink is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via the App or email. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
