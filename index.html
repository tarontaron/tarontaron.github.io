Scroll Face Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Scroll Face ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application Scroll Face (referred to as the "App"). By using the App, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect:

  • Camera Access: Scroll Face requires access to your device's camera to detect and track facial movements. This access is solely used for the purpose of enabling the scrolling functionality within the App.

How We Use Your Information:

  • Facial Recognition: We use the camera access solely for the purpose of detecting facial movements to enable scrolling functionality within the App. We do not store, transmit, or analyze any images or facial data captured by the camera.

Data Sharing:

  • No Data Sharing: Scroll Face does not collect, store, or share any personal data or information with third parties. We do not engage in any form of data sharing or selling of user information.

Data Security:

  • Security Measures: We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.

Children's Privacy:

  • Age Limitation: Scroll Face is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you become aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove that information from our systems.
