Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 02.04.2024

Thank you for using our app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and retain face data obtained through our application.

1. Collection of Face Data

Our app utilizes facial recognition technology to detect certain facial features, including eye open percent pitch angle coordinates. This data is collected in real-time while the user interacts with the application.

2. Use of Face Data

The collected face data is solely used to enable features within the app, specifically for controlling scrolling and swiping functionality through head movements. We do not use this data for any other purpose.

3. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share face data collected by our app with any third parties. All information remains confidential and is not disclosed to external entities.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Face data is stored securely on our servers and is retained only for the duration necessary to provide the intended functionality of the app. Once the data is no longer needed, it is promptly deleted from our systems.

5. Privacy Policy Information

Our privacy policy outlines our app's collection, use, disclosure, sharing, and retention of face data. The relevant sections pertaining to face data are as follows:

"We collect facial data, including eye open percent pitch angle coordinates, in real-time to enable scrolling and swiping functionality through head movements within the app. This data is solely used for this purpose and is not shared with any third parties. We store this information securely and retain it only for as long as necessary to provide the intended functionality of the app."
